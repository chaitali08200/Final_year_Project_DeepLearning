Skin Cancer Detection Using Deep Learning

Skin cancer is one of the most common forms of cancer and is rising globally. It is considered as one of the most dangerous types of cancers and there is a drastic increase in the rate of deaths due to lack of knowledge on the symptoms and their prevention. Early detection at premature stage is necessary so that one can prevent the spreading of cancer. Not many platforms provide an easy and effective way to detect skin cancer. Our project can help greatly by reducing the cost and time required for detecting the skin cancer which can lead to an earlier treatment.
